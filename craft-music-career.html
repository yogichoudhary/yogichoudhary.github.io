THE YIELD OF MUSICAL LAND .

  • Music is the fabric of our society, and music can shape abilities and character.
  • Students in band or orchestra are less likely to abuse substances over their lifetime. Musical education can greatly contribute to children’s intellectual development as well.
  • The rhythm of music has healing abilities that at any point in life is important. In terms of revenue, music may not yield very high return on investments but it surely yields a lot of inexpensive benefits that in monetary terms, cannot be measured.
  • The infotainment and entertainment industry has a special place for musicians, from evening events to title tracks if films, the scope of music is vast.

THE MULTIPLE AVENUES OF MUSIC INDUSTRY

THERE IS A LOT TO ROAM AROUND WHEN THE TALK IS B ABOUT MUSIC INDUSTRY, WE’LL COVER 5 OF
THEM IN THIS BLOG.

  1. MUSIC PRODUCER – The art of crafting the perfect frame for the birth of  music. The producer holds the maximum responsibility on his shoulders. Skills - foundational audio and music concepts, various types of software. 
  2. RECORDING ENGINEER – Looking into technicalities whilst a take is in  process is the major job. If the beats and audio aren’t in tandem, the whole track could sound hoarse. Skills - multiple recording technologies, file management skills, mixing and editing. 
  3. SESSION MUSICIAN – The one behind the instrument is the connoisseur of his  art. This is the time wherein he showcases the talent on others’ albums. Probably this is the first big step! Skills – improvisation, communication skills, industry knowledge. 
  4. ARTIST MANAGER – Behind every successful artist is his/her manager. Imagine  having all our assignments in head but not knowing the submission date, this will create a hassle at every minute! The artist manager gets the job right here. Skills – Management and Leadership skills. 
  5. COMPOSER – Music notations and recordings lubricate the further process of  delivering a hit track. Composing is the underbelly of every favourite line of your favourite song! Skills – Patience, distinction ability, Creativity.

And the list is too long! What is noteworthy is, hidden beneath each role is the sense of self development that each of these roles offer, not just better musicians but better and more enhanced minds!

THE MATH BEHIND THE PEACE OF MIND

REASONING ABILITY - The patterns of music, help calm the chaotic mind and increases the capability of solving complex questions.

MENTAL EQUILIBRIUM - Research shows that the brain produces brainwaves from music with a strong and defining beat, and those brainwaves determine our level of concentration and alertness, according to an article in the psychological health magazine Complete Wellbeing
.
COMPANIONSHIP - For times when we fail to calculate the complexities of life, music serves as a gateway to paradise. It often substitutes the friend who may not pick your call! Music floods the brain with a chemical called dopamine. Dopamine is the chemical in the brain associated with pleasure, motivation and reward.

CONFIDENCE DEVELOPMENT - It’s a great tool for distraction and redirecting our focus. It educates us and opens our minds to the common human experience, our history and our hope for the future. Music therefore can be used as a mood enhancer or elevator.

EMOTIONAL INTELLIGENCE - You may be surprised to learn that music gives rise to two different kinds of emotions, commonly referred to as felt emotions and perceived emotions. Your thoughts must revolve around about how it is possible to perceive the emotions without actually feeling them? This is because listening to music does not make us feel threatened or endangered. Hence, we incline towards smarter decisions.
