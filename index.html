About us

Established in 2014 in Vasant Vihar-Thane West, Mumbai, Sangeeta Academy, is the best player in Mumbai for Music Classes Category. Our class Sangeeta Academy began with a vision of mutual passion of our family and staff for music, creative arts, brain-enhancing strategies and academics. Our class started with a goal of contributing our staff’s expertise to those willing to take their skills to greater levels. Our esteemed staff who are professionally qualified for sharing their knowledge and are more happy to do so for those interested in learning.

This popular institution serves customers both locally and from other parts of Thane, Mumbai, as a one-stop destination. We have built a strong presence in the industry over the duration in this journey. Believing that customer satisfaction is as essential as their products and services, this institution has managed to help to gain a huge base of customers that continues to increase day by day. The aim of these classes is to extend its product and service line and to reach a wider customer base. The classes we offer are accredited and we ensure that during their learning phase our students are given beneficial skills.
